Who I am

I'm Kotryna — a freelance graphic designer with 10 years of experience making things look good. Logos, packaging, brand books, illustrations, murals, book layouts — you name it. If it needs a visual identity, I'm your person. I collaborate with advertising agencies and work directly with clients who care about design that's smart, a little bold, colorful, and impossible to ignore.

My process

01

Discover

We talk about your project and I send you a questionnaire to fill in — so I know exactly who you are, what you need, and what you love before I ever open a file.

02

Design

I create thoughtful, crafted visuals with attention to every detail — and always present a few different directions so you can choose what feels right, not just take it or leave it.

03

Deliver

Clean final files in any format you need — print, screen, whatever works for you. I can even liaise with printing companies on your behalf so you don't have to figure that part out alone.
